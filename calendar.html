The Néeark Year is 224 days long which most Sentient species have broken into 8 months, each consisting of 4 weeks. Each week is split into 7 days, historically named for one of the major Gods, with the 3rd day named in honor of the creation of Néeark, which was created in 3 days, according to myth.[1]

Current Date: 27th of Rainmoot, 673 (Age of Steel)

The Priodomian Calendar

The Priodomian Calendar was first utilized by the settlers who would found Kyria Pol in the Age of Bone. By the early days of the Age of Steel, the calendar was used across all of Néeark by the majority of the sentient species of the world, despite its name.

 

Important Dates

Holidays

  • Winter's Wake - 1st of Frostmoot: Celebrated as the beginning of the year and the winter season.
  • Midwinter's Night - 14th of Frostmoot: A night of feasting and revelry, celebrated as a halfway point between the winter solstice and the spring equinox.
  • Springtide - 1st of Deepsnow: A celebration of the return of spring and the coming of new life.
  • Festival of the Sun - 1st of Highsun: A celebration of the summer solstice, with feasting and outdoor activities.
  • Harvest Festival - 14th of Firewane: A time to celebrate the harvest season and give thanks for the bounty of the land.
  • Day of the Ancestors - 1st of Redfall: A day to honor and remember ancestors and loved ones who have passed away.
  • Festival of Lights - 14th of Redfall: A night of celebration and illumination, with lanterns and candles lighting up the streets.
  • Winter's End - 28th of Fellnight: A celebration of the end of winter and the beginning of the new year, with hopes for a bountiful year ahead.
