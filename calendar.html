The Néeark Year is 224 days long which most Sentient species have broken into 8 months, each consisting of 4 weeks. Each week is broken into 7 days, historically named for one of the major Gods, with the 3rd day named in honor of the creation of Néeark, which was created in 3 days, according to myth.[1]

Current Date: 26th of Rainmoot, 673 (Age of Steel)

The Priodomian Calendar

The Priodomian Calendar was first utilized by the settlers who would found Kyria Pol in the age of Bone. By the early days of the Age of Steel, the Calendar was used across all of Néeark by the majority of the sentient species of the world, despite its name.
