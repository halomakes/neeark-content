Rose

Race: Human

Pronouns: She/Her

Appearance: Rose wears long black hair with long fringes in each sides, pale skin and charcoal gray eyes. Her usual outfit is a dark blue high-neck long sleeved leather armor short dress with gold accents. She wears matching thigh-high boots and long gloves.

Bio: Rose was once a Hunter within the guild before an injury prevented her continuing in that capacity. Always a hunter with a keen eye for detail and monster's behavioral patterns, Rose was quickly offered the role of handler, working to assign contracts to appropriately skilled hunters in her “on hours” and studying monsters and monster activity reports in her “off hours”.

Naenasera

Kymil Willowwind

Race: Wood Elf

Pronouns: He/Him

Appearance: A young, handsome elf with deep black hair and piercing green eyes.

Bio: Kymil was the captain of the guard until the 23rd of Winterwane, 673, when he was killed by a Nargacuga which had been infected by an at-the-time unknown virus. Kymil died protecting Naenasera from the rampaging Nargacuge, and was given a hero's burial within Daso, an Elm planted to mark his body.

Lias Duskpool

Race: Wood Elf

Pronouns: She/Her

Appearance: Broad shouldered, and very tall for a Wood Elf, Lias sports long black hair which is usually kept in a tight, intricate braid. Her fierce facial features are often consciously offset by a soft smile, unless she is deep in thought or concentration.

Bio: Lias is a lieutenant in the Naenasera Guard, and was one of the guards who responded when Curd's entourage defeated a pair of hyper-aggressive Zinogre.
