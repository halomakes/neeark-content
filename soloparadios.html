Overview

This page is meant to be an “Invite Only” page to help me keep track of all the bullshit I'm making up but don't want the players to see. Seriously, please don't spoil yourself if you somehow found your way here (It's not exactly hard to find). I'll try to fill with some random bullshit before getting to spoiler territory, but if you scroll down that's on you.

I will also work to keep the Headings as Spoiler-free as possible. All the links are also riddled with, well…

So why am I writing all this shit down in the third person like I'm expecting an audience? I dunno, equal parts ego and thinking maybe the players, or other DMs will find this repository of ramblings, tools, homebrew, and notes about my made up world somehow useful. Maybe someone will want to steal an NPC or a storyline, and I think that's cool. Or maybe folks will just wanna make fun of my stupid Monster Hunter meets Ebberon world, and that's fine, too. Either way, I will write these as though there may some day be an audience for the ramblings, even if it's just some random stoner who found this in a haze of indica smoke and curiosity.

First Steps

First season was a bit of a mix, trying to get a feel for the world. Some canon was established, and the basic mechanics used throughout the campaign were introduced.

At the beginning of the second season, while keeping the next chapter in the minds of the players, I aim to get everyone used to things again, after the sabbatical, including the improvements I've introduced to the campaign. Things start off with an investigation into a proto-Elder Dragon that's been causing a bunch of shit, allowing for a few callbacks to season 1 of the campaign to solidify the canon.

Feywild Fuckery

Lia's auntie is starting some shit in the Feywild. During their exploits, the players have encountered agents of the Queen of Air and Darkness, whether they realized it or not. Some of Lia's blood was taken, allowing the Queen to initiate some fuckery which knocked one of the only two people who can stop her. Without her partner, Titania would be a match for the newly crowned “Unseelie Empress” who seeks to unite the Feywild under her banner, were it not for the God who has empowered her.

With extradimensional powers, the age of the Unseelie Empire may well be at hand…

The Cracking of the World

Upon their return from the Feywild, the party finds that a lot more time has passed than they would have expected. Now a decade later, much has changed, former allies are lost, and the course of Néeark seems to have been thrown wildly off course. The party must determine what has happened, and if there is any chance to save the world they left.
